Welcome

On behalf of the Organizing Committee of the Asia Pacific International Conference on Business, Economic, Science and Technology (APIC-BEST), it’s our privilege and pleasure to welcome you to 1st edition of this annual meeting. We are hosting this event in-person and we’re very much looking forward to your broad participation from Asia and all around the world.

The lively interaction amongst academic, industrial, and public sector stakeholders is essential to the success of such a critical area to society and we’re proud to provide an international forum for presentation and discussion of peer-reviewed research, development and implementation thrusts.

Our conference theme for this year is Post Pandemic Economic and Social Recovery Challenges, and how economy and society progressing in post pandemic era and what challenges society and economy are facing in current business environment. We’re proud of our selection of keynote speakers who represent the rich breadth of this field.

We would like to thank the tremendous efforts of our Organizing Committee, the technical program committee, reviewers and other volunteers who have generously donated their time and knowledge.

We warmly invite you to participate in the APIC-BEST 2022 and are confident that you will find the program enriching, enlightening and rewarding.

Submission Guidelines

The format of the paper should follow the APA formatting style. You can download a template and style guide. Click Here

APIC-BEST 2022 will only accept the electronic submission in Microsoft Word or PDF format of a full paper in English. Conference papers are limited to 8000 words and can be extendable to 9000 words. APIC-BEST 2022 proceedings will be published under the designated ISBN number.

The selected top 20% papers published in the APIC-BEST 2022 proceedings will be invited to submit their presented conference papers for publication in the Journal of Business Studies (JOBS) within four weeks of the conference final day. The submitted manuscript will go through another seperate review according to JOBS review process.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Submission of full papers (only A4 size will be accepted)

For online Paper submission

Ready to submit? Click Here for EasyChair Link and submit full paper along with author details.

For email submission

Ready to submit? Email full paper along with author details on craft.submission@nu.edu.pk

The author can adopt any of the above submission methods.

Step 2: Notification of Paper Acceptance

Once the review committee has changed the status of the papers to accepted, authors will be able to register for the conference. After the registration is complete, authors can complete the copyright form. Authors that are conditionally accepted will need to make the revisions required and then resubmit their papers.

Step 3: Submission of final papers after revision

Upload the final paper after revision on easychair or email the final paper after revisions on craft.submission@nu.edu.pk

Step 4: Presentation of papers at APIC-BEST 2022

Accepted papers must be presented at the conference by one of the authors. Presentation time is 12 minutes with 3 minutes for Q&As.
